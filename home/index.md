**New to D&D or AL?**
No prep needed --- bring yourself;
we’ll help you
roll up a character
and get you to the table.
**Veteran Adventurer?**
Jump into the action,
meet local players,
or even pitch in behind the DM screen.

**Play when you can, skip when you can’t**:
[Adventurers League](https://www.adventurersleague.info) is
D&D’s official organized play program:
the same rules and adventures running at
game stores, conventions, and online tables
worldwide.
Your character keeps
their level,
gold,
and gear
between sessions,
and travels with you
to any other AL table.

We play every 1--3 weeks
at [Bacchus and Venus](https://www.bacchusandvenus.com).
At our table,
scheduling lives on Discord —
and so do
resources for getting started.
