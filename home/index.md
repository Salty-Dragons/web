# Salty Dragons {#main .plain}

_A community for Dungeons & Dragons organized play in Sausalito._

We meet semi-weekly at [Bacchus and Venus](https://www.bacchusandvenus.com)
to play [D&D 5.5e](https://www.dndbeyond.com/sources/dnd/br-2024) under
[Adventurers League](https://www.adventurersleague.info) (AL) rules.
New to D&D or AL? No prep needed — bring yourself, we’ll help you roll up
a character and get you to the table.

Adventurers League is D&D’s official organized play program — the same
rules and adventures running at game stores, conventions, and online
tables worldwide. Play when you can, skip when you can’t: your character
keeps their level, gold, and gear between sessions, and travels with you
to any other AL table.

At our table, scheduling lives on Discord — and so do resources for
getting started.
