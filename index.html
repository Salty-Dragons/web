Salty Dragons

A community for Dungeons & Dragons organized play in Sausalito.

We meet semi-weekly at Bacchus and Venus to play D&D 5.5e under Adventurers League (AL) rules. New to D&D or AL? No prep needed — bring yourself, we’ll help you roll up a character and get you to the table.

Adventurers League is D&D’s official organized play program — the same rules and adventures running at game stores, conventions, and online tables worldwide. Play when you can, skip when you can’t: your character keeps their level, gold, and gear between sessions, and travels with you to any other AL table.

At our table, scheduling lives on Discord — and so do resources for getting started.
